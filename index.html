About the product

What is LabSync?

Inspectorio is a US-based cloud SaaS used by 12,000+ customers — including top global brands and retailers — to enhance Quality and Compliance operations.

LabSync connects brands, retailers, suppliers, and third-party labs across the supply chain. It standardizes lab testing data points (typically stored differently by each lab), centralizes test requests and reports in one place, and unlocks an analytics module that had never existed in the industry before.

Customers
Brands, Retailers, Suppliers & Factories — apparel, hard goods, soft goods
Revenue model
Subscription — Standard, Professional, and Enterprise tiers
Key partners
Lab agencies, Target, DICK'S Sporting Goods, Crocs, internal product & sales teams
Value proposition

Standardize & centralize lab testing — across every supplier, every lab, every market.

The existing process is fragmented — each vendor works with multiple labs storing data differently. LabSync standardizes data points and centralizes test requests, then layers a powerful analytics module on top — a capability that had not been available to users previously.

Process

How we built LabSync.

Built using Agile & Scrum with 2-week sprint cycles starting on Wednesday — chosen intentionally to avoid Monday sluggishness and long-weekend absences.

Lessons learned

Key takeaways.

01
Global clients need tailored communication.

Scheduling for multiple time zones, using their terminology, and explaining demos slowly ("like talking to a baby") avoids misunderstandings and builds trust.

02
Involve developers early — not just in code.

Bringing devs into customer interviews and design sessions from day one unlocks their full expertise and reduces costly late-stage changes.

03
Alignment first — 5W1H before execution.

Every cross-functional effort needs Who, What, When, Where, Why and How defined clearly upfront. Ambiguity compounds quickly in large teams.
