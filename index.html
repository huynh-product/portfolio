My story

From business student
to product builder.

I graduated with a Bachelor's in International Business Economics from Foreign Trade University, GPA 3.6. My product career started by accident — I applied for a warehouse role, and a sharp boss saw something else in me.

I've spent 6+ years building products across B2B SaaS, B2C travel, and social platforms — starting at VeXeRe where I built a cargo management SaaS for 500+ bus operators and launched a flight platform that drove 130% traffic growth MoM. Then at Golden Equator, I led Swish — an invite-only professional social network on iOS, Android, and Web — growing MAU 20%+ in 3 months.

At Inspectorio, I spearheaded 4 major products: Responsible Sourcing Compliance, Digital Assets, LabSync, and Traceability. I work directly with global brands — Walmart, Target, Tesco, DICK'S Sporting Goods, Crocs — and was honoured as Employee of the Year and 2× Hackathon Champion. I leverage AI extensively to extend my scope into design and engineering, accelerating delivery and raising quality.

Outside work, I founded Product Buddy — a knowledge-sharing community that has helped 40+ Vietnamese freshers land their first BA/PO roles through mentorship, real case studies, and practical curriculum.
